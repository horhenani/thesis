Challenges in Mobility

  1. Inefficient dispatching leading to uneven tricycle distribution
  2. Inconsistent and sometimes unfair fare calculation
  3. Difficulties in booking tricycles, especially during peak hours
  4. Safety concerns, particularly for vulnerable groups
  5. Lack of real-time tracking for emergency response and performance monitoring
  6. Absence of a centralized system for managing tricycle operations
